Our Vision and Mission Statement

To raise a Christ-like Generation that will manifest all their potentials in Christ Jesus.

What we do

  • We win souls
  • Disciple Believers
  • Minister to needs by the Supernatural Power of God.

We achieve this by

  • Preaching and Teaching a Balanced Doctrinal Diet that cuts across: Revival, Holiness, Purpose, Prosperity, The Supernatural, Faith, Prayer, Wisdom,Leadership, Influence, Family Life, and Ministry.
  • We Emphasize the Place of Prayer as the Core Secret of an Effective Christian Life
  • We allow the Ministeration and flow of the Spirit and Power of God
  • We Enlist, Train and Mentor an Efficient Workforce
  • We Provide Counselling and Mentorship to Many
  • We have an outreach arm, via our Itenerant Ministry Platform
  • We have Social Media Platforms that reach thousands beyond our physical domain


In the last 7years of our ministry, since it's inception

  • 100s ( hundreds) have been saved,

  • 1000s have been mentored and imparted
  • 1000s have been healed, delivered and miraclously imparted

Our Next Phase

God has given us a mandate to start a Branch to give a better and stronger expression to multitudes domicile at the other end of the city. Unlike our present students' predominantly based location - close to the Federal University of Technology, Minna, we would have a platform that ministers more to the entire City and beyond.

The Bosso branch of the ministry will yet retain it's meeting days as friday evenings,while the new Tunga, Minna branch will hold on Sunday evenings

To kickoff services at our new Tunga branch, we have the following plans

  • Have a 40 Forty Nights Prayer chain
  • Carry out training for our workforce
  • Setup the chosen location putting in place all necessities to make the venue convenient for the congregration
  • Carry out a city wide publicity, through Billboards, Flyers, and Social Media promotions
  • Provide Bus Shuttles at strategic locations of Minna city for attendees.
